Ta-Wei Tu 塗大為

I am currently a research intern in the Department of Algorithms & Complexity (D1) at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), mentored by Professor Danupon Nanongkai. Prior to that, I got my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at National Taiwan University (NTU).

I am interested in theoretical computer science in general, with a focus on algorithms and data structures for graph problems and combinatorial optimizations.

I will be joining Stanford CS as a Ph.D. student in the Theory Group in Fall 2023!
