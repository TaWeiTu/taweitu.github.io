Ta-Wei Tu 塗大為

I am a first-year computer science Ph.D. student in the theory group at Stanford University, advised by Professor Aaron Sidford.

Before joining Stanford, I was an intern in the Department of Algorithms & Complexity (D1) at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), where I was mentored by Professor Danupon Nanongkai. I got my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from National Taiwan University in 2022.

I am interested in theoretical computer science in general, with a focus on the design of algorithms and data structures for graph problems and combinatorial optimizations.
